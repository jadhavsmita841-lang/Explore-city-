By Road
Satara has a functional bus stand.you also have the option of availing Volvo/reclining seat/sleeper buses.private operators providing bus services.Apart from private buses,MSRTC(Maharashtra state Road Transport Corporation) offer regular and frequent bus services to satara from Mumbai and Pune. the area also well connected to other neighbouring cities by public buses.
By Train
Situated on the Pune-Miraj line,the satara Railway Station is well connected to other indian cities.Regular trains from Mumbai and Pune are also available to reach Satara.
By Air
Lohegoan Airport is located at a distance of 123km from Satara and can be reached by buses and taxis available near the airport.it takes approximately 2 hours and 44 minutes to make the journey to satara.Mumbai's Chhatrapati Shivaji Airport is located at a distance of about 264 km from Satara,and is a four and a half hour's journey via road.
